To decide upon a suitable range of media to design for, via Google Analytics, I discovered the following sizes to be the most popular:

  1. 320x568
  2. 360x640
  3. 375x667
  4. 414x736
  5. 768x1024
  6. 1366x768

Some dimensions are considered standard, such as an iPad (768x1024), or a standard Android device (360x640).

With the dimensions I then created what I refer to as the Container Grid.

The web is constantly evolving, any list I create could eventually change and that may mean going back to the drawing board at a later date. What's worth noting is that my analysis of data—from the account of a large e-tailer—covered a period of 1 year & 1,000,000 users, during that period the most popular sizes did not change.

The experience could be specifically tailored towards existing customers, as opposed to users that probably won’t shop with us. Users of screen sizes not in the list will also receive an excellent experience due to the strategy of including fluid elements [examples needed, e.g. bleeding imagery fluid header and footer]. This gives us the benefits of designing for certainty, whilst creating the impression of fluidity.
