For the initial exercise I discovered the following sizes to be the most popular, via Google Analytics (listed in order of size):

  • 320x568
  • 360x640
  • 375x667
  • 414x736
  • 768x1024
  • 1366x768

Some of the sizes are considered “standard”, such as the screen size for an iPad in portrait orientation (768x1024), or the size for a standard Android device (360x640). The web is constantly evolving and that could affect this kind of list; accept that things could change in future. However, my analysis covered over 1 year and the list of popular sizes did not change during that period.
